## Session Overview

**Topic:** AI-powered conversational recommendation engine for an all-in-one architecture & home solutions e-commerce platform serving architecture designers and high-end homeowners.

**Goals:**
- Conversational Discovery — AI agent (chat/voice) that uncovers what customers truly need
- Dynamic Persona Building — Construct customer profiles in real-time from discovery conversations
- Persona-Driven Recommendations — Serve products based on the built persona
- Continuous Persona Refinement — Evolve personas based on recommendation feedback loops
- Long-Term Memory — Remember individual customer preferences across sessions

### Session Setup

_Platform covers: furniture, shower equipment, kitchen equipment, home products, building materials, electrical, design services, and parking solutions. Target audience includes architecture designers and high-end homeowners. The system should feel like a casual conversation that naturally surfaces customer needs and preferences._

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** AI-Recommended Techniques
**Analysis Context:** AI recommendation engine for architecture platform with focus on conversational discovery, persona building, and long-term memory

## Technique Execution Results

### Role Playing — Consumer Homeowner Archetype

**Interactive Focus:** First-time homeowner who is excited but overwhelmed, lacks design vocabulary, and knows what they don't want more easily than what they do want.

**Key Breakthroughs:**
- Customers cannot articulate preferences in design language — the system must never require it
- Elimination (rejection) is cognitively easier than selection — flip the discovery model
- Emotional state (excitement + anxiety) matters at first interaction — validate before asking
- Speed-to-value is critical — too long in discovery mode and customers bounce
- The persona itself is the retention hook — "we remember you" is the reason to return

## Session Summary and Insights

**Key Achievements:**
- 34 ideas generated across 6 themes from 3 creativity techniques
- Clear technical architecture emerged (embedding-based, multi-faceted personas)
- Practical build sequence defined from foundation to advanced features
- Core UX philosophy established: elimination over articulation, speed over completeness

**Breakthrough Concepts:**
- The customer's inability to describe what they want is not a problem to solve — it's the design constraint to embrace (elimination-first)
- The recommendation engine and chat agent are not separate features — they're one system operating in the same vector space
- The taste profile bar is simultaneously a UX element, a gamification tool, and a retention mechanism
- The same system that serves customers also informs business procurement decisions

**Design Principles Emerged:**
1. Never require design vocabulary from consumers
2. Recommend within 60-90 seconds — speed-to-value above all
3. Every interaction is a persona signal — nothing is wasted
4. The persona is multi-faceted, evolving, and never "complete"
5. Stated preferences and behavioral signals are both valid but weighted differently over time
